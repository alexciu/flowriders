Get Started


Be Safe

A great rider never gets injured. Safety is out number 1 priority. Our teaching methods guarantee that you will not get hurt and learn snowboarding in the safest way possible.

Have Fun

That's why you're here for right? We choose a slope based on your level of experience, to make it challenging, but also doable. There are many options, and the levels go from beginners to off-piste. We guarantee a smile on your face at the end of the session.

Learn

Snowboarding is easy to learn with proper guidance. For beginners it's best not to try and learn on their own, as bad habits are hard to forget. For more experienced snowboarders it's best someone helps when learning something new.
